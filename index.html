About Me

I’m Mudavath Kalyan, a full-stack developer with a passion for crafting smart, scalable, and AI-integrated web applications. With a strong foundation in the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js), I’ve expanded my expertise into cutting-edge technologies like Next.js 15, Prisma, NeonDB, and Google’s Gemini API.

My latest venture, ProPathAI, is an AI-powered career guidance platform offering personalized resume reviews, mock interviews, and tailored learning roadmaps. I thrive on building projects that solve real-world problems and improve user experiences.

Whether it's building robust backends, designing intuitive frontends, or integrating AI to create smarter systems — I'm all in. I’m constantly learning, building, and pushing the limits of what’s possible with modern web technologies.
